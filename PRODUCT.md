# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Tchurch serves church staff, ministry leaders, volunteers, and members who manage services, assignments, songs, events, announcements, giving, and team communication from mobile and tablet devices.

## Product Purpose

The product helps a church coordinate operational work in one app: plan services, view assignments, manage ministry details, publish updates, and keep members connected. Success means users can complete common church workflows quickly on iPhone and comfortably on iPad without feeling like the interface is merely stretched.

## Brand Personality

Calm, organized, welcoming. The interface should feel trustworthy and task-focused, with enough warmth for a church community tool and enough restraint for repeated administrative use.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-site composition inside authenticated workflows, oversized decorative panels, phone-only layouts stretched across tablet screens, cramped touch targets, and tablet views that waste space with a single narrow column when a two-column structure would clarify the task.

## Design Principles

- Preserve mobile confidence: every iPhone flow remains thumb-friendly, readable, and safe-area aware.
- Use tablet space structurally: prefer split views, wider grids, and balanced content rails over stretched cards.
- Keep repeated work quiet: navigation, lists, forms, and modals should be predictable and scannable.
- Make role-sensitive tools obvious: admin and leader affordances should be visible without making the app feel heavy.
- Protect real-world usage: touch targets, loading states, and empty states need to hold up during live ministry planning.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use WCAG AA contrast as the baseline, keep interactive targets at least 44px on touch devices, support dynamic safe-area insets on iPhone and iPad, and avoid motion that blocks task completion.
